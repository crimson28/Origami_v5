Origami Facts :


  • Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper).
  • For the initial 1500 years or so of its existence, origami was actually known as orikata!
  • Back in 100 AD when it was first made, paper was an expensive and rare commodity, so those engaging in the art of traditional paper folding was usually an art left for the elite rungs of society.
  • As a good luck token for all the future dangers to be faced, Samurais commonly used to present gifts of origami to each other.
  • The most common and well known folded object is a paper crane
